# 📊 Food Delivery Data Analysis & Dashboard (Excel)

## 🔹 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of a food delivery dataset using **Microsoft Excel**. The goal is to extract meaningful business insights related to revenue, customer demand, delivery performance, and operational efficiency.

The project includes **data cleaning, transformation, and visualization**, along with an interactive dashboard to support data-driven decision-making(Data:16k+).

---

## 🔹 Objectives

* Analyze revenue patterns across cuisines and restaurants
* Identify peak order days and customer demand trends
* Evaluate delivery performance and efficiency
* Detect potential business risks (slow delivery, low satisfaction)
* Provide actionable recommendations for business improvement

---

## 🔹 Tools & Techniques Used

* **Microsoft Excel**
* **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts**
* **VLOOKUP & HLOOKUP**
* **Conditional Formatting**
* **Data Filtering & Sorting**
* **Data Cleaning & File Handling**
* **Dashboard Design with Slicers**

---

## 🔹 Data Processing

* Cleaned and structured raw data for analysis
* Handled missing values and inconsistencies
* Created new calculated columns:
* Customer Satisfaction
* Delivery Efficiency

---

## 🔹 Key Features

* 📈 Revenue analysis by cuisine
* 📅 Order trends by day
* 🚚 Delivery time performance by restaurant
* ⚠️ Identification of slow delivery and risk areas
* 🎛️ Interactive dashboard with slicers for dynamic filtering

---

## 🔹 Key Insights

* Revenue is distributed across multiple cuisines, showing diverse customer preferences
* Order volume is higher during peak days (e.g., weekends)
* Some restaurants show slower delivery times, indicating operational inefficiencies
* No major customer satisfaction risk observed, but delivery optimization is needed

---

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

* Improve delivery efficiency for slower restaurants
* Leverage high-performing restaurants for express delivery programs
* Optimize resource allocation during peak demand periods
* Focus on high-revenue cuisines for marketing and promotions
